SHINCHAN MOVIE

Story: The movie takes place at Dino's Island, a theme park in Tokyo where dinosaurs have been revived. Shinnosuke and his friends are visiting the park when Shiro encounters the small dinosaur named Nana. They become friends, and the story follows their adventures as dinosaurs escape from the park.
Themes:The movie emphasizes the importance of friendship and connection.
Creative Team:The movie is directed by Shinobu Sasaki and written by Moral.
Release Date:It was released in Japan on August 9, 2024. In India, it was released on May 9, 2025.
